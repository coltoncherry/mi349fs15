Sandskiff

Sandksiff by velcrast on Sketchfab

The insipiration for the Sandskiff comes from Borderlands 2. I wanted to do the sandskiff with a twist by trying to envision what the Sandskiff would have looked like if constructed by Hyperion.

Merkava Mk IV

Merkava Mk4 by velcrast on Sketchfab

The inspiration for doing the Merkava Mk IV came from wanting to do some hard surface modeling and deciding that doing a tank would be good practice. At first I had planned on building the PL-01, but couldn't find any good orthographic views. So for the first tank I have ever done I chose a Merkava Mk IV.
